Lagos, Nigeria

Lagos is a state in the southwestern Nigeria, on the coast of the Bight of Benin. It is bounded by the state of Ogun to the north and east, by the Bight of Benin to the south, and by the Republic of Benin to the west.Lagos is a major African financial centre and is the economic hub of Lagos State and Nigeria at large.Lagos is the most populous city in Nigeria as well as Africa with an estimated population of 21 million in 2015.
